Questionnaire 5.0.1 supporting Moodle 5.0 and 5.1 has been released. Details are in the ["CHANGES" file](https://github.com/PoetOS/moodle-mod_questionnaire/blob/v5.0.1/CHANGES.md). The release can be found on [Github](https://github.com/PoetOS/moodle-mod_questionnaire/releases/tag/v5.0.1) and the [Moodle plugins database](https://moodle.org/plugins/mod_questionnaire/5.0.1-build-2025110900/38654).

This release alse includes all of the new features, improvements and fixes from release 4.4.0.
